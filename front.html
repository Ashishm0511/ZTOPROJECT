FAQs

Find answers to your questions about registration, eligibility, and competition format here.

How do I register?

You can register online through our official website. Simply fill out the registration form and submit it. Make sure to complete this process before the deadline.

Who is eligible?

Eligibility varies by grade level. Typically, students from grades 1 to 12 can participate. Check the specific criteria on our eligibility page.

What is the format?

The competition consists of multiple-choice questions and problem-solving tasks. Participants will have a set time to complete the exam. Detailed guidelines will be provided prior to the event.

When does it start?

The Zonal Talent Olympiad is scheduled to begin in early March. Specific dates will be announced on our website. Keep an eye on our announcements for updates.

Where will it take place?

The competition will be held at various designated centers across the region. Locations will be confirmed after registration closes. Participants will receive detailed information via email.
